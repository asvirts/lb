About Linton

Linton is a member of the Republican Party running to be the District 9 Representative of the Lafayette Parish School Board, representing Youngsville, Milton, and Broussard.

A conservative with a background in Accounting, Finance, and Banking, he will ensure proper management of our tax dollars. As a father, pastor, and substitute teacher for Lafayette Parish Schools, he knows our community and the needs of our students and educators. Linton will fight for what's best for all students and their families, as well as for educators and the growing communities he represents in South Lafayette Parish.

Linton is married to Rachel Lacy Broussard and together they have 3 children, one adult son and two sons currently attending and thriving in Lafayette Parish Public Schools.
