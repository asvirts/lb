About Linton

Linton is running for the Board Member position in District 9 which consists of Youngsville, Milton, and a portion of Broussard.

His educational background in Accounting and Finance as well as his experience as a pastor and a substitute teacher for Lafayette Parish Schools will aid him in fighting for what's best for all students, especially those in District 9.

Linton is married to Rachel Lacy Broussard and together they have 3 sons, two of which are currently enrolled in the Lafayette Parish School System.
